Four Dimensions of Communication

Your communication style is shaped by how you express yourself across these key areas:

💬

Verbal Communication

What you say and how you express ideas

  • 🗣️ Speaking patterns and word choice
  • ✍️ Writing style and precision
  • 👂 Listening and comprehension approach
  • 📚 Information processing preferences
🎵

Paraverbal Communication

How you deliver your message

  • 🔊 Volume and tone variations
  • ⚡ Speaking pace and rhythm
  • 🎭 Vocal inflection and emotion
  • 🏛️ Formality level in speech
🤝

Body Language

Non-verbal expression and presence

  • 👀 Eye contact and facial expressions
  • 🤲 Gestures and hand movements
  • 🚶 Posture and body positioning
  • ✋ Physical touch preferences
🏠

Personal Space

Environment and spatial preferences

  • 💼 Workspace organization style
  • 📏 Physical distance preferences
  • 🖼️ Personal item display choices
  • 💻 Virtual environment setup
